About

A Space for Understanding & Healing

As a highly experienced psychotherapist, I offer a safe and supportive environment where you can explore life's challenges and make sense of what feels difficult.

My work is grounded in psychodynamic principles, which means we look together at how your past experiences, unconscious processes, and relationship patterns may be shaping your present life. This exploration can lead to greater self-understanding, healing, and lasting change.

A Welcoming Space for All

I offer therapy in both English and Farsi, and I welcome clients from all cultural, religious, and spiritual backgrounds including Jewish, Christian, Muslim, and secular communities.
